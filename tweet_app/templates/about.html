* This app classifies whether a Twitter user's recent tweets (up to 3200 tweets) have been majority positive or negative and how sure the classifier is of this prediction.

* The classifier uses a logistic regression model trained with word-count feature vectors.

* The app filters out any tweets with no English words.

* The classifier was trained using English tweets labeled positive and negative.

* Positive and Negative word lists used are from:

Minqing Hu and Bing Liu. "Mining and Summarizing Customer Reviews."
Proceedings of the ACM SIGKDD International Conference on Knowledge
Discovery and Data Mining (KDD-2004), Aug 22-25, 2004, Seattle,
Washington, USA

Bing Liu, Minqing Hu and Junsheng Cheng. "Opinion Observer: Analyzing
and Comparing Opinions on the Web." Proceedings of the 14th
International World Wide Web conference (WWW-2005), May 10-14,
2005, Chiba, Japan.
